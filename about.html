A little bit about me

I'm a UX/UI designer from Thornhill, Ontario, Canada, with a background in graphic design. I'm passionate about creating thoughtful experiences and visually pleasing interfaces. I also enjoy illustrations and cartoons.

As a designer, I've always had a desire to understand how human behavior and empathy drive experience design. I enjoy working as a creative, but I wanted to create something that is highly functional based on feedback.

I enrolled in a UX/UI Bootcamp at the University of Toronto and learned the concepts and processes from research to development. I've applied my learnings through various projects in the course including mobile apps and website redesigns.
